# Market Map: EU Pre-purchase Car Inspection

## Context
Market: European Union (focus on GE, SE, BE, NL, IT, PL, LT).
Model: B2B (aggregators/dealers) → B2C (individual buyers).

## Market Segments

### 1. B2B (Primary Clients)
- **Used Car Dealers/Re-sellers:** Need trust indicators for remote buyers.
- **Leasing Companies:** End-of-lease inspections or remarketing prep.
- **Car Export Brokers:** Buying cars in DE/NL for export to Eastern Europe or Central Asia.
- **Online Car Marketplaces:** Need verified status for listings.

### 2. B2C (End Users)
- **Local Buyers:** Lack technical knowledge to inspect a car themselves.
- **Remote Buyers (Cross-border):** Buying a car in another EU country without traveling.
- **Premium Buyers:** Buying expensive/luxury cars where the cost of error is high.

## Jobs To Be Done (JTBD)

### "Verify for me" (The Skeptic)
- **Situation:** Found a car, seller says it's perfect, but I don't trust them.
- **Motivation:** Avoid buying a "lemon" or a car with hidden accidents/odometer fraud.
- **Outcome:** Peace of mind or reason to walk away.

### "Be my eyes" (The Remote Buyer)
- **Situation:** Car is 500km away in another country.
- **Motivation:** Save time and travel costs.
- **Outcome:** Professional report with photos/videos via WhatsApp to decide if it's worth the trip/purchase.

### "Help me get a better deal" (The Optimizer)
- **Situation:** Car is good, but I want to pay less.
- **Motivation:** Use found defects as leverage for price reduction.
- **Outcome:** Lower purchase price covering the inspection cost.

## Market Trends
- **Digitalization:** Moving from paper reports to instant messenger/web-based dashboards.
- **Transparency:** High demand for paint thickness measurement and OBD scans (where possible).
- **Cross-border trade:** Increasing volume of used cars moving from Western to Eastern/Southern EU.
