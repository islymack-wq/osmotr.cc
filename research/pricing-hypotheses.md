# Pricing Hypotheses & Service Tiers

## Package Hypotheses

### 1. Basic / "The Quick Check"
- **Focus:** Bodywork and basic functionality.
- **Includes:** Visual inspection, paint thickness measurement (thickness gauge), photo report (30-40 photos), basic engine sound check.
- **Price Range:** €100 – €150.
- **Best for:** Budget cars or initial filtering of multiple options.

### 2. Standard / "The Full Report" (Flagship)
- **Focus:** Comprehensive technical and condition assessment.
- **Includes:** Basic + OBD Scanner (if allowed by seller), test drive (as passenger or driver), interior condition check, identification number (VIN) check.
- **Price Range:** €180 – €250.
- **Best for:** Most private and dealer purchases.

### 3. Premium / "The Concierge"
- **Focus:** High-end cars and remote peace of mind.
- **Includes:** Standard + Video report (walkaround + engine start), fluid quality check, lift inspection (if service station is nearby and available).
- **Price Range:** €300 – €450.
- **Best for:** Luxury cars, sports cars, or classic cars.

## Add-ons (Upsells)

- **Price Negotiation (€50 - €100 or % of saving):** Expert negotiates with the seller on behalf of the client based on found defects.
- **Urgent Inspection (+€50):** Guaranteed inspection within 24 hours (SLA reduction).
- **Legal/History Check (€30 - €50):** Database check for accidents, theft, and mileage history (CarVertical/Autovcheck integration).
- **Transport Arrangement (Quote-based):** Coordination with logistics partners to deliver the car.

## Important Notes & Caveats
- **Travel Costs:** Prices assume the car is within the inspector's city radius. Surcharge for long-distance travel (€0.50 - €1.00 per km).
- **Scanner Limitations:** Disclaimer that OBD scanning might be blocked by the seller or not possible on certain models/conditions.
- **VAT:** Prices should be clarified as Gross (incl. VAT) for B2C and Net for B2B.
