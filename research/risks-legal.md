# Risks & Legal Analysis

## 1. Compliance & Sanctions
- **Risk:** Inspecting cars intended for export to sanctioned regions (e.g., Russia/Belarus) via middlemen.
- **Mitigation:** Implement a "Customer Due Diligence" (CDD) check for B2B clients. Avoid involvement in the actual export/logistics paperwork if possible.
- **Risk:** GDPR compliance when taking photos of cars (license plates) and communicating via WhatsApp.
- **Mitigation:** Standardize the masking of license plates in reports. Use a professional WhatsApp Business API account with clear Privacy Policy.

## 2. Liability & Responsibility
- **Risk:** Customer sues after buying a car that develops a major fault 1 week after inspection.
- **Mitigation:** Clear "Limitation of Liability" in Terms of Service. State that the report is a "snapshot in time" and not a warranty or insurance policy. Liability capped at the price of the service.
- **Risk:** Inspector damages the car during inspection or test drive.
- **Mitigation:** Require all inspectors to have professional liability insurance or personal car insurance that covers test drives.

## 3. Quality & Fraud
- **Risk:** Inspector colludes with the seller to hide defects for a bribe.
- **Mitigation:** Random "shadow" audits. Cross-referencing findings with car history reports (VIN). Promptly removing experts with suspicious "always clean" reports.
- **Risk:** Refund abuse (customers claiming the report is "bad" just to get money back).
- **Mitigation:** Clear definition of "Standard Report" quality. Refunds only for missing mandatory checklist items, not for "subjective" disagreements.

## 4. Operational Risks
- **Risk:** Seller refuses entry or becomes aggressive.
- **Mitigation:** SOP for inspectors to leave immediately if threatened. Fee structure that compensates the inspector for their time/travel even if the check is aborted.
- **Risk:** Dependency on WhatsApp (platform risk).
- **Mitigation:** Develop a simple web-based backup for report delivery and image hosting.

## 5. Insurance
- **Recommendation:** Explore "Professional Indemnity Insurance" for the platform itself to cover potential legal claims from unhappy buyers.
