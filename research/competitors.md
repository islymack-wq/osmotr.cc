# Competitor Analysis Categories

## Category 1: Traditional Inspection Agencies (TÜV/DEKRA style)
- **Characteristics:** Highly standardized, physically rooted, often bureaucratic.
- **Candidates:** TÜV SÜD/Rheinland, DEKRA (Germany/EU), SGS (Global).
- **What to check:** Speed of scheduling, report format (usually PDF/Paper), price point (usually high).

## Category 2: Digital-First Inspection Platforms
- **Characteristics:** Aggregators of freelance inspectors, focus on UX and speed.
- **Candidates:** HUK-AutoService (DE), various local startups in PL/LT.
- **What to check:** Network density, mobile app usage, booking flow.

## Category 3: Marketplace Integrated Services
- **Characteristics:** Services offered directly inside AutoScout24, Mobile.de, or eBay Motors.
- **What to check:** Exclusive partnerships, trust badges on listings.

## Category 4: Specialized Export Inspectors
- **Characteristics:** Focus on buyers from outside the EU or cross-border.
- **Candidates:** Individual "podbor" (car selection) services on Instagram/YouTube/Telegram.
- **What to check:** Trust levels, payment methods (often informal), report depth.

## Strategic Checklist for Further Research
1. **Pricing models:** Flat fee vs. tiered vs. percentage of car price.
2. **Standardization:** Do they use a unified checklist or let the inspector decide?
3. **Response time:** How fast from payment to report?
4. **Added value:** Do they offer negotiation, transportation, or legal help?
5. **Liability:** What happens if they miss a major defect? (Terms & Conditions review).
