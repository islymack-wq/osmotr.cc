# Interview Guide (25 Questions)

## Block 1: For Ilya (Founder/Internal)
1. What is the biggest bottleneck in the current process of scaling from 50 to 100 experts?
2. What is the most common reason a report is rejected by our Quality Control?
3. How do we currently verify the honesty of our inspectors (preventing them from taking "bribes" from sellers)?
4. What percentage of sellers refuse to allow an OBD scanner?
5. Which country in the current list (GE, SE, BE, NL, IT, PL, LT) has the highest demand vs. lowest supply?
6. What is the average distance an inspector has to travel to a car?
7. What is the most requested "add-on" service that we don't officially offer yet?
8. How do we handle situations where the car is sold between the booking and the inspection?

## Block 2: For B2B Clients (Dealers/Export Brokers)
9. What is your primary goal when ordering an inspection for a car you intend to resell?
10. How many inspections do you order per month on average?
11. What is the maximum price you are willing to pay for a standard inspection to keep your margins healthy?
12. Is a WhatsApp report sufficient, or do you need a formal PDF for your accounting/clients?
13. How critical is the 2-day SLA? Would you pay more for a 4-hour SLA?
14. What are the top 3 car defects that are "deal-breakers" for your business?
15. Do you prefer a "Pass/Fail" recommendation or just raw data/photos?
16. How do you currently handle car history checks (VIN)? Would you prefer it integrated?

## Block 3: For B2C Clients (Remote/Local Buyers)
17. How did you find the car you want us to inspect?
18. What is your biggest fear regarding this specific purchase?
19. Have you ever bought a car remotely before?
20. Why did you choose a professional service instead of asking a friend or going yourself?
21. How important is it for you to speak directly with the inspector after the check?
22. Would you be interested in a service where we also handle the money transfer to the seller?
23. What information in the report would give you 100% confidence to buy the car?
24. How do you feel about the current price point of [X] Euros for this service?
25. If the report says the car is "bad", will you use us again for the next car? (LTV question)
